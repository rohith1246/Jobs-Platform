from groq import Groq
from jobmatch import JobScraper, ResumeParser

agent = JobScraper(
  sources=["linkedin", "naukri", "indeed"],
  location="India, Remote",
  ai_filter=True
)

resume = ResumeParser("my_resume.pdf")
matches = agent.match(resume)

What It Does

Everything. Automated.

From scraping to scoring, the entire job search is handled by AI so you focus only on applying.

Multi-Platform Scraper
Crawls LinkedIn Guest API, Naukri, and Indeed in parallel. Finds hundreds of real job URLs every run.
🧠
AI Job Parser
Groq LLaMA reads each job page and extracts title, company, skills, salary and more — structured JSON every time.
📄
Resume PDF Matcher
Upload your resume PDF once. The AI scores every job from 0–100% based on your actual skills and experience.
🎯
Fit Score Engine
Each job gets an AI explanation — why you're a fit or not, and a personalized outreach pitch ready to send.

PostgreSQL Storage
